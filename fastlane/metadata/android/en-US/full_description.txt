<i>Translate You</i> is a Translator App built with <a href="https://m3.material.io/">Material Design 3 (You)</a> using the <a href="https://github.com/LibreTranslate/LibreTranslate">LibreTranslate API</a>. It supports more than 30 languages out of the box while owning an innovative design. It doesn't connect to any non-open-source directly and can therefore increase the user's privacy.
